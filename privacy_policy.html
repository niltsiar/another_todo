Privacy Policy for Another Todo App

Last updated July 20, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Another Todo App ("we," "us," or "our") operates its mobile application, Another Todo App (the "App").

Another Todo App is designed for simplicity and privacy. It allows you to create and maintain your todo items with no complex features. All your data is stored locally on your device and is not synced to any cloud service. The app does not request any special permissions, including internet access.

SUMMARY OF KEY POINTS

This summary provides key points from our Privacy Policy:

What personal information do we process? We do not collect any personal information, including usage data, from you or your device when you use our App. All data you create within the App (your todo items) is stored exclusively on your device.

Do we process any sensitive personal information? No, we do not process any sensitive personal information.

Do we collect any information from third parties? No, we do not collect any information from third parties.

How do we process your information? We do not process your information as no personal information is collected by the App.

In what situations and with whom do we share personal information? We do not share any personal information with third parties, as we do not collect any personal information through the App.

What are your rights? Depending on where you are located geographically, the applicable privacy law may mean you have certain rights regarding your personal information. As we do not collect personal information, these rights primarily relate to your ability to manage data directly on your device.

How do you exercise your rights? If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or believe we may have unintentionally collected data, please contact us at the email address provided below. We will consider and act upon any request in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

In Short: We do not process your information as no personal information is collected by the App.
